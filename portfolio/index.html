Aurora Bank Insights

Click to expand

The Aurora Bank Data Challenge focuses on leveraging customer, transaction, and card data to drive data-driven decision-making. The mission is divided into two key analytical directions:

1. Customer Profiling & Segmentation:

  • Analyze customer demographics (age, gender, location) to tailor marketing strategies
  • Assess credit scores and their influencing factors (income, debt, age)
  • Evaluate financial health using metrics like debt-to-income ratios
  • Study card ownership and usage trends

2. Transaction and Risk Analysis:

  • Examine spending patterns by merchant categories
  • Track geographical trends in transactions
  • Monitor transaction errors to improve user experience
  • Flag high-value transactions for fraud detection

Data Professional Survey Breakdown

Click to expand

Project description:

This project aims to analyze survey data from data professionals to uncover insights into their career paths, job satisfaction, compensation, and priorities within the industry. By examining responses on role titles, career transitions, salary ranges, industry representation, and various aspects of job satisfaction (such as work/life balance and growth opportunities), this project seeks to provide a comprehensive view of the experiences and motivations of data professionals. Additionally, demographic details like age, gender, and country add depth to the analysis.

Goals:

  • Identify Career Trends: Understand the most common job titles, industries, and demographics among data professionals.
  • Evaluate Job Satisfaction: Explore satisfaction levels across factors like salary, work/life balance, coworkers, and upward mobility.
  • Uncover Career Transition Insights: Assess how many professionals switched into data from other fields and the challenges they faced.
  • Highlight Job Search Priorities: Determine what factors (e.g., salary, growth opportunities) are most important to data professionals when considering new positions.
